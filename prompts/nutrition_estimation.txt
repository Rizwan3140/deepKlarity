Nutrition Estimation Prompt (embedded inside recipe_extraction prompt)
=======================================================================

This prompt section instructs the model to estimate nutritional values
based solely on the ingredients extracted from the recipe text.

Prompt excerpt used in the LLM chain:
--------------------------------------

"nutrition_estimate": {
  "calories": <integer — approximate calories per serving based on ingredients>,
  "protein": "<value in grams>g",
  "carbs": "<value in grams>g",
  "fat": "<value in grams>g"
}

Rules applied:
- Estimate values per serving (divide total by servings count).
- Base estimates on standard nutritional databases (USDA averages).
- Round calories to nearest 10; protein/carbs/fat to nearest gram.
- If certain ingredients are ambiguous (e.g., "a pinch of salt"), ignore their macros.
- Do NOT hallucinate ingredients not present in the recipe.
- Label format: "25g" (no space between number and unit).
