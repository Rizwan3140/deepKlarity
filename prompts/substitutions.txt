Ingredient Substitution Prompt (embedded inside recipe_extraction prompt)
=========================================================================

This prompt section instructs the model to generate 3 ingredient substitutions
for dietary preferences, allergies, or ingredient availability.

Prompt excerpt used in the LLM chain:
--------------------------------------

"substitutions": [
  "Replace <original ingredient> with <substitute> for <reason/benefit>.",
  "Replace <original ingredient> with <substitute> for <reason/benefit>.",
  "Replace <original ingredient> with <substitute> for <reason/benefit>."
]

Rules applied:
- Generate exactly 3 substitutions.
- Each substitution must reference a real ingredient that appears in the extracted recipe.
- Cover at least one of: dietary restriction (vegan, dairy-free, gluten-free),
  healthier alternative, or ingredient availability substitute.
- Substitutions must be realistic and commonly known.
- Do NOT suggest substitutions for seasonings (salt, pepper) unless there is a dietary reason.
- Format: "Replace X with Y for Z benefit."
